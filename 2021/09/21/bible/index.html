十五世纪德国纽伦堡圣经摇篮本单页2张
Fifteenth Century German Nuremberg Bible Incunabula (2 Pages)

单页纸张尺寸约：310× 215mm
Single Page Approximate Size: 310×215mm

1485-86年印刷于德国纽伦堡的《圣经》摇篮本单页。由著名雕刻大师丢勒的教父、印刷商兼出版家安东·柯贝尔格（Anton Koberger)承印，本页主要内容来自《约翰福音》（中央部分为正文）。
During 1485-86, the Holy Bible incunabula was printed in Nuremberg, Germany by printer and publisher Anton Koberger — the godfather of the famous engraver Dürer. The main content of this page is from The Gospel of John (the central part is the main text).

正文部分为哥特体大号 铅活字印刷，每句开头字母为红色墨水涂饰。 周边为75行圣经注释， 由著名神学家 Nicholas de Lyra（公元1270 1340年）所著。
The main text is printed in large blackletter, and the beginning letters of each sentence are painted in red ink. Surrounding it are 75 lines of Bible commentary, written by the famous theologian Nicholas de Lyra (1270 1340 AD).

状态如图，500多年的摇篮本古籍单页，段落开头有手工上色的描红，优质手工棉纸，对开本。有水渍，整体保存状态良好。
The state of the pages is shown in the pictures. With more than 500 years of history, the hand-colored red tracings at the beginning of each paragraph are clear. The pages was made by high-quality handmade cotton paper. Although there are few water stains on the side of the pages, their overall condition remain good.
